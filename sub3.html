CHINCHINBAR : a series of Italian, contemporary restaurants, found in world cities with varied and
different identities. Sharing a culinary philosophy that rides the wave of the seasons with creativity,
elegance, humour and sensuality, and from where they draw their inspiration.
Capturing the youthful energy embodied in Gucci and its parallels with the philosophy ,
they draw from the thread of legacy,always contemporary in its present and driving forward to
the future.
